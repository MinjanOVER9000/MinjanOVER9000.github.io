The History and the Orign of the legendary game......
Chess

The most commonly held belief is that chess originated in India, where it was called Chaturanga, which appears to have been invented in the 6th century AD.
Although this is commonly believed, it is thought that Persians created a more modern version of the game after the Indians.

Objectives
of Chess

The objective of the game chess is to capture as many of your opponents pieces and place the king under a checkmate, which is a state for the opponent where they cant prevent their king from being captured.

Pieces
of Chess

There are many different kinds of pieces in the game and there are two different sides, White and Black. The different pieces are in the picture on ur right (left to right) Pawn, Rook, Knight, Bishop, a queen, and the most important piece in the game...
The King.
